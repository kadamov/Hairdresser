About Us

Lystrup Frisor is a hairdressing salon which is located in a small city near Aarhus, named Lystrup. It is located 4 minutes walk from the center of the city. The salon open doors before 3 years and the start was not so difficult by reason there were not many other competitors in the area and customers start coming and raising day by day until now. Today Lystrup Frisor is one of the most popular hair salons in this hood. It is a place where you can get not only a fashion haircut but also to spend a great time during nice talks and drinking good coffee.
